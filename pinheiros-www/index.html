Pinheiros festival od Ceramic Design is held in Caldas da Rainha, city significant in portuguese pottery tradition because of it’s ceramic factory
and the figure of Rafael Bordalo Pinheiro. Thanks
to the initiative of students of Escola Superior de Artes e Design from 2008 the school and city’s musuems host the unique festival.
Our main objectives are:

- promote young ceramic artists & designers

- create a platform of thought and knowledge   exchange

- make opportunity for students to meet significant   ceramics creators

We believe that Festival will become a significant annual cultural event.		 

Rafael Augusto Bordalo Pinheiro is one of the most famous and successful Portuguese ceramists in history. Born in Lisbon into a family of distinguished artists, he received his first lessons in drawing and modeling in his father's studio.

In 1883, his brother persuaded Rafael to lend his artistic talent to a ceramics factory in Caldas da Rainha, he invested in. Rafael immediately plunged
into modeling in clay. A very rare example of his earliest work is shown in Plate VIII. He assumed the artistic direction with such vigor that the factory achieved high-quality production almost from the
start. The company exhibited at the Portugal Com- mercial Exhibition in Lisbon in 1885.
Pinheiro’s style is original, although inspired by Ma- nueline- and Palissy-type ceramics. He managed to combine exquisite elegance with the need to pro-
duce ergonomic, everyday use ceramic pieces.
